About Us

A Creative Hub Rooted in Community

DeonM Art Gallery (PTY) Ltd is more than just an art gallery—we are a multi-functional creative hub serving eMakhazeni (Belfast), Mpumalanga. Founded on the belief that art should be accessible, professional, and transformative, we bridge the gap between fine art excellence and commercial creative services.

From hyperrealistic pencil drawings and oil paintings to commercial branding, custom apparel printing, and youth art education, we offer comprehensive creative solutions. Our mission is to nurture artistic talent while providing professional services that meet the diverse needs of our community and beyond.

Deon Mahlangu

Founder & Creative Director | Fine Artist & Entrepreneur
