About Us

Where Art Meets Soul

Deon Art Gallery is a fine art creator and creative brand dedicated to producing original works that resonate with emotion and authenticity. Founded by Deon Mahlangu, we specialize in custom portraits, original artwork, professional framing, and artistic consultation.

Our approach combines traditional fine art techniques with contemporary African creative identity, resulting in pieces that are both timeless and uniquely personal. Every artwork is crafted with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring that your vision is brought to life with the highest level of artistry and professionalism.

Deon Mahlangu

CEO, Founder & Managing Director | Fine Artist
